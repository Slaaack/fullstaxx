## What to Look for in an AI Tool for Real Estate

Before spending anything, be honest about where your time actually goes. For most agents, the biggest drains are:

- **Lead response time** — buyers and sellers expect replies within minutes, not hours
- **Listing descriptions** — writing them is tedious and they all start to sound the same
- **Follow-up sequences** — most leads don't convert on first contact; you need consistent nurture
- **Market reports and CMAs** — clients want data, fast
- **Admin and scheduling** — back-and-forth coordination that eats hours every week

The best AI tools address at least two of these. Tools that only do one thing — like AI headshots or virtual staging — are nice-to-haves, not necessities.

### 2. Structurely — Best AI for Lead Qualification

Structurely does one thing and does it exceptionally well: it uses an AI chatbot (named "Holmes") to qualify leads via SMS and email before they ever reach you. Holmes carries on realistic two-way conversations, captures intent, and flags the leads most likely to convert.

It integrates with most major CRMs (Follow Up Boss, Salesforce, HubSpot) so you don't have to rip and replace your existing setup. If you're running paid ads and drowning in unqualified leads, Structurely is the fastest way to reclaim your time.

**Where it shines:**
- Natural-feeling AI conversations that don't read like a bot
- Integrates with existing CRM and workflow
- Great for high-volume lead gen (Zillow, Realtor.com buyers)

**Where it falls short:**

### 3. ChatGPT (Plus) — Best for Content and Listing Descriptions

ChatGPT isn't real estate-specific, but with the right system prompts, it becomes one of the most useful tools in your stack. Agents use it to:

- Write listing descriptions in seconds from a bullet-point brief
- Draft buyer/seller newsletters and drip email sequences
- Generate neighborhood market summaries
- Create social media captions for new listings
- Draft offer cover letters and client update emails

The key is building a library of prompts that match your voice. A well-crafted listing description prompt that includes property details, target buyer persona, and tone guidance will produce near-publish-ready copy in under 60 seconds.

**Where it shines:**
- Fastest way to eliminate listing copy writer's block
- Incredibly versatile — content, research, templates, objection scripts
- $20/month for GPT-4o (or free with limitations)

**Where it falls short:**
- Requires prompt discipline — garbage in, garbage out
- No real estate-specific integrations or MLS data
- You still need to fact-check and personalize output

### 4. Rechat — Best for Teams and Brokerages

Rechat is a marketing and CRM platform built specifically for real estate teams. Its AI tools focus heavily on marketing automation: it can generate branded listing flyers, social posts, email campaigns, and property pages from MLS data with one click.

For solo agents, it may feel like more than you need. But for a team of 5–15 agents sharing a brand, it becomes a serious efficiency multiplier. The AI auto-populates templates from listing data, so your agents aren't fighting Canva at midnight before an open house.

**Where it shines:**
- One-click marketing from MLS data
- Consistent branding across a whole team
- Solid CRM features baked in

**Where it falls short:**
- Expensive for single agents ($199+/month)
- Best value comes from team-level usage
- The CRM isn't as AI-forward as Lofty

## Do You Actually Need a Paid AI Platform?

Honest answer: most agents under 20 transactions per year don't need Lofty or Structurely right out of the gate. Start here instead:

1. **ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo)** — Build a listing description prompt, a drip email sequence prompt, and a market summary prompt. Use them every week.
2. **Otter.ai ($16/mo)** — Transcribe every client call. Pull notes into your CRM.
3. **A solid CRM** (Follow Up Boss, KVCore, etc.) with built-in automation — most have basic AI features already included.

That's a $36/month AI stack that will meaningfully improve your output. Once you're closing 20+ transactions and drowning in leads, then a platform like Lofty makes sense.

## What to Avoid

A few categories worth skipping:

- **AI headshot generators** — fine for a social profile pic, not a business needle-mover
- **"AI-powered" tools that are just keyword stuffers** — watch for products that slap "AI" on basic automation
- **Virtual staging apps with no trial** — results vary wildly; never pay without testing on your own photos first
