A great CRM won't just organize your contacts; it'll remind you to follow up at the right moment, automate your drip campaigns, and give you a clear view of your pipeline.

We spent three months testing the top real estate CRMs, including Follow Up Boss, LionDesk, HubSpot, Wise Agent, and CINC. Here's what we found.

## 1. Follow Up Boss — Best Overall for Real Estate Teams

Follow Up Boss has been the gold standard for real estate CRMs for years, and the 2026 version cements that reputation with better automation and a refreshed mobile app.

### What we love

- **Lead routing** is genuinely powerful — automatically assigns leads to agents based on source, area, or round-robin
- **Integrations** with every major lead source: Zillow, Realtor.com, BoomTown, Opcity, and 200+ more
- **Two-way texting** built in, with templates and auto-responses
- **Team transparency** — managers can see all agent activity, follow-ups, and pipeline health in one view

### What could be better

- Pricing is steep for solo agents ($69/month)
- The UI has a slight learning curve — plan for an hour of onboarding
- No built-in AI assistant (yet)

### Pricing

| Plan | Price | Users |
|------|-------|-------|
| Grow | $69/mo | 1–2 users |
| Pro | $499/mo | Up to 10 users |
| Platform | $1,000/mo | Unlimited |

**Best for:** Teams of 3+ agents who need accountability and lead routing.

## 2. LionDesk — Best Budget CRM for Solo Agents

At $25/month, LionDesk punches well above its weight. It includes built-in calling, texting, video email, and AI-powered texting features that competitors charge a lot more for.

### What we love

- **AI Text Response** — LionDesk can automatically respond to leads via text using AI, which is remarkable at this price point
- **Video email** — send personalized video messages directly from the CRM
- **Transaction management** — basic deal tracking built in

### What could be better

- Interface feels dated compared to Follow Up Boss
- Team features are limited on the base plan
- Reporting is basic

### Pricing

LionDesk starts at **$25/month** for solo agents. The Pro+ plan at $49/month adds AI features and more integrations.

## 3. HubSpot CRM — Best Free Option to Start

If you're just getting started and not ready to commit to a paid CRM, **HubSpot's free CRM** is legitimately excellent. You get contact management, deal tracking, email sequences, and a pipeline view — completely free, forever.

The downside is that real estate-specific features (like lead source integrations with Zillow) require HubSpot's paid Sales Hub, which gets expensive fast.

**Best for:** Agents just starting out who want to learn CRM habits before investing in a specialized tool.

## How We Tested

We created accounts for each CRM, imported the same 100-contact dataset, and tested:

1. **Contact organization** — tagging, segmentation, custom fields
2. **Lead capture** — web forms, Zapier integration, lead source sync
3. **Communication** — email, calling, texting from within the CRM
4. **Automation** — drip sequences, reminders, follow-up tasks
5. **Reporting** — pipeline views, conversion tracking, team performance

We also surveyed 42 active real estate agents on their daily workflow and pain points.

## The Bottom Line

- **Best overall:** Follow Up Boss (if you can afford it)
- **Best value:** LionDesk for solo agents on a budget
- **Best free:** HubSpot CRM to start without commitment

Whatever you choose, the most important thing is to **actually use it**. A $25/month CRM you use every day beats a $500/month one you ignore.
